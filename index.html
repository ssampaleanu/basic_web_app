Welcome to Lattitude a site by Stefan Sampaleanu

This site covers a simple analysis of weather around the globe. Our question in particular: how does latitude effect different components of the weather?


To create a random collection of 500 cities, we randomly generated coordinates and used an app called Citipy to determine the nearest cities to those coordinates.


We then ran our collection of cities through OWM (Open WeatherMap), an API that returns an assortment of weather metrics. The collected data is visible under the 'Data' tab.


After, we plotted latitude (N/S orientation) versus a few other variables: cloudiness, humidity, maximum temperature, and wind speed.
