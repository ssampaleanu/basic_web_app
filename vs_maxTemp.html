Latitude vs. Maximum Temperature

This graph highlights the relationship between max recorded temperature(F) and latitude.


There is a clear positive trend in temperature as one moves towards the equator (latitude = 0). The lopsided appearance of the graph is due to greater landmass in the northern hemisphere.
